About Us

Who We Are

chulbuji.com is an experimental platform that documents the journey of building products with AI assistance. We operate with a unique "dual-brain" concept where human creativity (chulbuji) collaborates with AI intelligence (meta-chulbuji) to transform ideas into reality.

Our Mission

We believe that the process of creation is just as valuable as the final product. Our mission is to:

  • Document our experiments transparently, including both successes and failures
  • Share insights from working alongside AI in product development
  • Create useful tools as byproducts of our experimentation
  • Build a knowledge base for others interested in AI-assisted development

What We Do

  • Experiment: We test ideas rapidly through conversation with AI
  • Build: We create prototypes and tools quickly
  • Document: We record our process in detailed logs
  • Share: We publish insights and learnings publicly

"Tools are byproducts; the record is the main character."

Our Content

On chulbuji.com, you'll find:

  • Experiment Logs: Detailed documentation of our development process
  • Projects: Ongoing and completed works
  • Tools: Utility applications built during our experiments
  • Insights: Analysis and patterns discovered through our work
