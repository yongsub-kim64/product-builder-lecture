From Lotto Sample to Record Hub

Starting today, chulbuji.com is no longer a "lotto number generator sample." We are becoming a record hub that turns thoughts into structure, and structure into action.

Why the change?

This site initially started as a "web tech demo." The lotto generator was a practice sample for JavaScript. However, the real value was not in the tool itself, but in the process of creating the tool.

  • Structuring ideas through conversations with AI (meta-chulbuji)
  • Rapidly creating and testing prototypes
  • Learning by recording failures and successes

This process itself is the content. That's why we are transforming into a site where "the record is the main character."
