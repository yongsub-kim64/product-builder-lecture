## 05-A. Payment and Subscription Data Processing

### Payment Information Processing
1. Payment information is processed through third-party payment processors (e.g., Toss Payments, Stripe, PayPal).
2. **Information stored on this site**: Last 4 digits of card number, card issuer name, payment authorization number, transaction timestamp
3. **Information NOT stored**: Full card number, CVV, passwords
4. Payment processors are PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) certified.

### Auto-renewal Processing
1. Explicit consent for auto-renewal is obtained when subscribing.
2. Renewal reminder emails are sent **7 days before** the renewal date, and email delivery records are retained.
3. Information processed during auto-renewal: Member identification, subscription status, payment method information (in tokenized form)
4. Auto-renewal consent can be withdrawn at any time, and related records are retained for 6 months after subscription ends for dispute resolution.

### Payment Failure Handling
1. Payment is retried up to 3 times upon failure, and the reason and time of each attempt are recorded.
2. Payment failure notification email delivery records are retained.
3. After 3 failed attempts, the subscription is automatically terminated, and such information is processed according to the retention periods above.

### Personal Information in Refund Processing
1. Information collected for refund requests: Refund reason (optional), request timestamp, processing status
2. Records are retained for 5 years after refund completion in accordance with tax laws and e-commerce regulations.
3. If additional identity verification is required during refund processing, only minimal information is requested.
